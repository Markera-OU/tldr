Privacy Policy - TL;DR Document Summariser

Markera OÜ provides its product, TL;DR Document Summariser, an add-on for Google Workspace, in accordance with this Privacy Policy. TL;DR Document Summariser provides a TL;DR summary of highlighted text in a document.

How We Collect Information

TL;DR Document Summariser requests permission to install the Google Workspace add-on, which includes accessing the highlighted text, an action taken by the user. The data provided (highlighted document text) is then used to provide a summary, and is not stored in any way or used for any other purpose. We may use third party APIs (openai.com) for utility purposes, and data is transmitted only for the purpose of processing related to the core functionality of summarising documents.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to provide the core functionality of the TL;DR Document Summariser – summarising documents by extracting important phrases and sentences. We do not store or use the data for any other purpose.

How We Disclose Information

We do not sell, rent, or disclose information we collect with third parties. We may, however, use text inputs against third party APIs (openai.com) for utility purposes and in order to process the summarising of documents.

Security of Information

We do not physically store any content that passes through our system. We have implemented security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data under our control.

Jurisdiction

TL;DR Document Summariser is provided by Markera OÜ, based in Estonia and subject to EU privacy regulations.
